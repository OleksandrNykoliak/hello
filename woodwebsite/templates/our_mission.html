Our Mission

Where craftsmanship meets commitment

Founded in 2017 in the heart of Tallinn, Estonia, SilverSword specializes in European wood species, offering a wide range of veneers and edging materials. From the rich texture of oak and the refined elegance of beech to the unique character of ash and cherry, our product line meets a variety of aesthetic and functional requirements.

Each piece of veneer and every meter of edging material is meticulously selected and processed to ensure premium quality and consistency. At SilverSword, we don’t just supply wood – we shape possibilities and bring ideas to life.

Our Purpose

We believe in the timeless beauty of natural wood. Our mission is to bridge modern design with the authenticity of nature by providing premium-grade materials that serve not only aesthetic ambition but functional excellence. We serve architects, designers, builders, and creators who value precision, sustainability, and a personalized approach.

Driven by Values

  • Excellence: We pursue perfection in every panel and veneer.
  • Sustainability: Responsibly sourced timber and eco-friendly production practices.
  • Innovation: Constant refinement of our materials and services.
  • Integrity: Transparent processes and reliable partnerships.

We’re more than a wood company — we’re your partner in creative transformation.

Join us in redefining what’s possible with European wood.
